Overview

Purpose

To provide a simple, easy to understand, and informative way to learn to code, play around with coding, and share what people have made.

Audience

People desiring a start to coding, somebody continuing their journey in coding, someone wanting to just play around with some coding, or someone wanting to view someone else's program that they made.

Typography

Heading Font: Play Bold

This font looks to me like a coding style font. Not one you would code in, but one that makes me think of code or coding. It is also bold and so it makes it seem more like a header font.

Paragraph Font: Nova Mono

This font is skinny, kind of fancy, and it also reminds me of coding. Though surprisingly, it could be used as a coding font, since it is monospace.
